MTA Time-Piece
PROJECT

PROBLEM:

Design an interactive app for the touchscreen devices in MTA Trainstations that incorporates time.

SOLUTION:

An interactive way for people in train stations to explore New York states native plants, and how they look throughout the year.

Brainstorming and Sketching

Part of the project was to trade ideas, so that you are able to work with designing an idea that isn't your. The idea that I was gien was as way to see animals throught all stages of life. Since this project was hypothetically for the MTA, which is an integral part of New York City, I decided to focus the project on New York rather than all animals.

Iteration 1

For my first iteration I decided to test out different way of displaying the data. I wanted to play around with different shades of green since this UI is all about nature.

Final Iteration

For my final iteration I chose the version that looked the most academic. I thought it looked academic because of the serif fonts, and the muted colors. I also made the view of time a circle based on feedback given by my professor. He mentioned that with plants some of them are perennial and some of the are annual, so having a cyclical view of time for plants could push that message across.
