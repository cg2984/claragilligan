VENDING MACHINE UI
PROJECT

PROBLEM:

Design a UI for a touchscreen vending machine that has an "Add to Cart" function..

SOLUTION:

I wnted to make a bubbly UI that easily encapsulated all the functions of a vending machine.

Wireframing

In this stage I began to sketch my ideas on paper and in illustrator for the project.

Refining Sketches

Since I had a lot of sketches, it was time to start refining them. I made my selections based on the feedback that I got from my classmates, and my professor.

Final Iteration

I narrowed my selection down to one type of logo and began iterating on the small details in tat logo. I eventually came to my final version because I liked the brandmark of the mars turning into the golfball. I also liked the goofy off-kilter lettering which I felt reflected fun.

Merchandise Application

Wehn applying the logo to a piece of merch, there were a few changes that I had to implement. Since I wanted to iron on my logo to a hat I had to change the background of the logo. The original logo has a transparent background, but this one has a purple background so that the logo is easier to cut out of the iron on paper. I chose purple because I thought that it was a nice complimentary color, and becasue I thought it had a similar space age fun feel.
