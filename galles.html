GALLE'S LOGO
PROJECT

PROBLEM:

Design a logo for a mini-golf company on Mars.

SOLUTION:

I wanted to make a logo that was fun and goofy, while incorporating visual elements of gold and of Mars. I chose the name Galle's because it is a crater on Mars that looks like a smiley face, so I thought it would be interesting to incorporate a smiley face.

Brainstorming and Sketching

In this stage I began to sketch my ideas on paper and in illustrator for the project. I wanted to play with a broad range of brandmarks and typography. At this stage I was experimenting between having golf visual elements and Martian visual elements. One thing that I thought about having was the smiley face because of the Galle's Crater, which is shaped like a smiley face.

Refining Sketches

Since I had a lot of sketches, it was time to start refining them. I made my selections based on the feedback that I got from my classmates, and my professor. THe feedback helped me refine the designs down. For the most part, people liked the smiley face and the golfball that turns into Mars.

Final Iteration

I narrowed my selection down to one type of logo and began iterating on the small details in tat logo. I eventually came to my final version because I liked the brandmark of the mars turning into the golfball. I also liked the goofy off-kilter lettering which I felt reflected fun.

Merchandise Application

Wehn applying the logo to a piece of merch, there were a few changes that I had to implement. Since I wanted to iron on my logo to a hat I had to change the background of the logo. The original logo has a transparent background, but this one has a purple background so that the logo is easier to cut out of the iron on paper. I chose purple because I thought that it was a nice complimentary color, and becasue I thought it had a similar space age fun feel.
